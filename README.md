# HR Analytics – Employee Attrition Analysis

This project was developed as part of a university data analytics assignment. The objective was to explore an HR analytics dataset and investigate the factors that may influence employee attrition.

The analysis combines exploratory data analysis (EDA) with statistical modeling techniques to better understand relationships between employee characteristics and the likelihood of leaving the company.

## Objectives

- Explore the structure and characteristics of the HR dataset
- Perform exploratory data analysis to identify patterns and relationships
- Apply statistical models to evaluate factors influencing employee attrition
- Interpret model results in a business analytics context

## Methods

The project uses two main analytical approaches:

**Multiple Linear Regression**
- Identification of relationships between HR variables and attrition-related metrics
- Verification of key regression assumptions:
  - Linearity
  - Homoscedasticity
  - Normality of residuals
  - Multicollinearity
  - Independence of errors
- Model refinement and interpretation of results

**Logistic Regression**
- Modeling the probability of employee attrition
- Evaluation of model fit
- Hypothesis testing and interpretation of coefficients
